Who are we?

We're a small family-owned flower shop. We began in the late 1980s in the comfort of our home and since then we've been expanding. Time has taught us a lot about the flower business and the details that truly make those special occasions special. We have built strong relationships with our vendors to ensure that whatever flower you desire is not only present during your special moments but also of optimal quality. We go above and beyond to provide you with the best customer experience possible. We don't just want to meet your expectations, we want to exceed them. From our careful selection of flowers to the proper delivery and maintenance of your flowers.

Why shop with us?

There are tons of other flower shops, so why us? From a socioeconomic perspective, you will be helping a small business and the local economy. You will also get the opportunity to talk to the people organizing and making your flowers. If you want something a particular way, we will make note of it. Having issues with something, we'll give you our two cents and our flexibility.

By going with us, a local shop, you are guaranteed proper care and attention to detail. Believe it or not, big commercial shops typically employ small businesses. In doing so, communication is lost between the customer and the flower designer, meaning that details are likely to be lost. Additionally, the big company takes a large chunk of the small shop's money, which can lead to lower quality control. If you hire us, you will not only get the level of detail you deserve but also collaborative feedback. Believe us. We've had a variety of customers and some things don't work quite well. We'll steer you in the right direction if something won't work as intended.
